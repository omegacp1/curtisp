About Bible Way Temple International


Church New Building dedicated in 2000

Bible Way Church is an established religious organization in the Morvant Community that have been in existence for over forty (40) years. Our vision and mission underscores the need to reach out to the society to help them find ways of improving and advancing their lives with an emphasis on Christian Family development

The new location was acquired as part of a uniting of two churches formerly located in 2nd Caledonia and St Joseph Roads Morvant. The Pastors, Bishop Herbert Marecheau and Apostle Celestine Paul both now deceased has laid the foundation for a thriving ministry to touch and transform the lives in our community
